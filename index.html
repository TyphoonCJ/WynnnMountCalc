Mount Feed Optimizer

Calculate optimal materials to max out your mount
How to Use
Step 1: Enter your mount's highest stat value - this determines the maximum material level you can use.
Step 2: For each stat, enter its current Limit and Max value.
Step 3: Click "Find Optimal Materials" to get the minimum materials needed to reach max on all stats.
Configuration
Mount Highest Stat:
Materials will be selected from the highest available level based on your mount's highest stat. The optimizer finds the minimum number of materials needed.
Mount Stats
Enter Limit and Max for each stat. If Stat is maxed leave blank or put in 0 works either way.
